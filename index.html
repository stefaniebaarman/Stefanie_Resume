About Me

Hi, my name is Stefanie Baarman and I am a Senior at Florida State University studying Information, Communication, and Technology with a minor in Computer Science.

I have a passion for web design, programming, and photography. My passion for technology began when I got my first camera in elementary school. I spent most of my days finding different ways to photograph simple objects. Along with the creative aspect of photography, I was always intruiged by how technology worked. Throughout middle and high school, I would watch YouTube tutorials on how to code in C++ and Java, to better understand computers.

In the future, I see myself utilizing my passions in creavitity and programming to implement technologies that will better our everyday lives. After graduating college, I plan to work for a start-up company or a digital agency as a User Experience Designer. Later on in my career, my goal is to work for a huge tech company such as Google or Apple. In addition, I want to work my way up to a leadership position such as a Lead Designer, or even a CEO.
